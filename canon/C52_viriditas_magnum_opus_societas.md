## 🟢 VIRIDITAS — The Greening Power

### Origin

**Hildegard von Bingen** (1098–1179) — Benedictine abbess,
composer, physician, mystic, theologian — placed at the center
of her entire cosmology a single concept she invented herself:

***Viriditas*** — from the Latin *viridis* (green) —
the **greening life-force** that pervades all creation.

> *“This vigor that hugs the world — it is warm, it is moistening,
> it is firm, it is greening — so that all creatures might germinate
> and grow.”*  
> — Hildegard von Bingen, *Liber Divinorum Operum* (1163)

> *“Viriditas is the natural driving force toward healing and wholeness,
> the vital power that sustains all life’s greenness.”*  
> — Hildegard

For Hildegard, Viriditas was not a metaphor.
It was the **literal animating force of the cosmos** —
the divine energy made visible as green in every living thing.

The Holy Spirit flowing through plants, animals, and elements.

(the Son) — and Viriditas (the greening) as the creative, immanent,

Hildegard saw that green was not just biological.
