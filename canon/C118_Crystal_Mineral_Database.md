## Crystal System Classification

All minerals are grouped by their crystallographic system — the geometry of their repeating unit cell. This geometry directly determines piezoelectric potential and resonance coupling.

| System | Symmetry | Piezoelectric Eligible | GAIA Role |

| Monoclinic | 1 symmetry plane | Weak | Memory Storage |
| Orthorhombic | 3 axes, no rotation | Moderate | Structural Anchoring |
| Tetragonal | 4-fold rotation | Moderate-High | Signal Amplification |

| Hexagonal | 6-fold rotation | High | Frequency Broadcast |
