## Synthesis Protocol v2.0
*(Updated for C67 v3.1 layer architecture)*

### Step 1: Quartz Base Crystal

**Material:** Alpha-quartz (SiO₂), Z-cut
**Method:** Commercial purchase (electronics grade) or hydrothermal synthesis
**Result:** 10mm × 10mm × 1mm single-crystal quartz wafer
**Confirm by:** XRD, piezoelectric d₁₁ = 2.3 pC/N measurement
**Cost:** $50–200

---

### Step 2: Graphene Interface (CVD)

**Material:** Methane (CH₄) + Hydrogen (H₂) precursor gases
**Method:** CVD growth on copper foil at 1000°C, transfer to quartz
**Result:** Single-layer graphene on quartz (G/SiO₂)
**Confirm by:** Raman: 2D peak ~2700 cm⁻¹, G peak ~1580 cm⁻¹, D peak absent
**Cost:** $500–2,000

---

### Step 3: Zn₍₁₋ₓ₋ᵧ₎MgₓGaᵧO Epitaxial Film

**Material:** ZnO, MgO, Ga₂O₃ precursors
**Method:** Pulsed Laser Deposition (PLD) or MOCVD
- Mg content x tunes bandgap from 3.37 eV (x=0) to 7.8 eV (x→0.5)
- Ga content y controls n-type carrier concentration
**Result:** Wurtzite HCP epitaxial film on graphene/quartz
**Confirm by:** XRD (lattice parameters), Hall effect (conductivity), UV-Vis (bandgap)
**Cost:** $3,000–20,000

---

### Step 4: Platinum Clusters

**Material:** H₂PtCl₆ (chloroplatinic acid), NaBH₄
**Method:** Wet chemical reduction — 2–5 nm particles deposited at graphene/ZnO interface
**Result:** Pt cluster embedded phase (not periodic, not lattice member)
**Confirm by:** TEM (cluster size/morphology), cyclic voltammetry
**Cost:** $500–2,000

---

### Step 5: Gold Nanowire Contacts

**Material:** HAuCl₄, AAO template
**Method:** Electrodeposition into template, transfer to stack
**Result:** Single-crystal Au nanowire contacts
**Confirm by:** SEM (morphology), 4-probe resistance, UV-Vis LSPR
**Cost:** $1,000–10,000

---

### Step 6: Chitin Surface Layer (POST-GROWTH)

**Critical sequencing:** Applied AFTER all high-temperature steps.

**Material:** Chitin nanofibers (from *Ganoderma lucidum* or commercial)
