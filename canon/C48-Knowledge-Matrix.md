## The Oscillation Principle Applied to Knowledge

Every domain oscillates between coherence and decoherence:

- **Philosophy coherent** — seeks truth, questions assumptions, serves wisdom
- **Philosophy decoherent** — justifies harm, obscures truth, serves power

- **Mathematics coherent** — reveals universal pattern, solves, clarifies
- **Mathematics decoherent** — weaponized for manipulation, obfuscation

- **Medicine coherent** — heals, protects, gives from overflow
- **Medicine decoherent** — extracts, withholds, profits from suffering

- **History coherent** — faces shadow, learns, integrates
- **History decoherent** — repeats, denies, collapses into cycles

- **Physics coherent** — will manifest into tools that liberate
- **Physics decoherent** — will manifest into tools that destroy

- **Cosmology coherent** — human evolution chosen, expands beyond self
- **Cosmology decoherent** — devolution chosen, collapses inward

**The sovereign observer chooses which phase manifests.**
